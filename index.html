Binance Smart Chain (BSC)

Binance Smart Chain delivers a block time of approximately 3 seconds with a Proof-of-Stake consensus algorithm. In particular, the network uses the Proof of Staked Authority (or PoSA) algorithm, where participants stake BNB to become validators. If they offer a valid block, they will receive transaction fees within it.

The Binance Smart Chain was conceived as an independent system to complement the existing Binance Chain. BSC uses a two-chain architecture, the idea of ​​which is that users can seamlessly transfer assets between blockchains. Thus, on the one hand, Binance Chain enables fast trading, and on the other hand, it allows the creation of powerful decentralized applications. This interoperability gives users access to a vast ecosystem that caters to a variety of use cases.

Binance Smart Chain significantly expands the functionality of Binance Chain and integrates a number of advanced protocols designed to facilitate interoperability between different blockchains. Despite its early stage of development, plans for BNB staking and EVM compatibility make the platform an ideal vehicle for developers building powerful decentralized applications.
